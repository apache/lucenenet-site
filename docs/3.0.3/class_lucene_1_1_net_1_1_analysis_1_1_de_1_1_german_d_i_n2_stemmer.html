Do some substitutions for the term to reduce overstemming:

  • Substitute Umlauts with their corresponding vowel: äöü -> aou, "&#223;" is substituted by "ss"
  • Substitute a second char of a pair of equal characters with an asterisk: ?? -> ?*
  • Substitute some common character combinations with a token: sch/ch/ei/ie/ig/st -> $/В&#167;/%/&/#/!
