Filters {@link StandardTokenizer} with {@link StandardFilter}, {@link LowerCaseFilter}, {@link StopFilter} and {@link SnowballFilter}. Available stemmers are listed in {@link SF.Snowball.Ext}. The name of a stemmer is the part of the class name before "Stemmer", e.g., the stemmer in {@link EnglishStemmer} is named "English".
